Software Blueprint

A successful software blueprint is similar to a blueprint for a house. It includes a formal requirements document outlining all the specs that the software project will contain. This is coupled with an actual mock-up of the website or mobile app. The blueprint that SourceBOLD will deliver you will be 100% yours. SourceBOLD will then help you build the website or mobile app.

Staff Augmentation

If you already have developers, but your staff is swamped, this service is for you. Whether you need a developer for an ongoing project or for just three months, SourceBOLD can help you by supplying outsourced talent. We will find the right skillset to augment your team and magnify the skills of its existing members. Hiring an outsourced developer has two advantages over a local, full-time employee. First, outsourcing saves money and is usually one-third the cost of hiring the same talent locally. Second, outsourcing is a great way to staff a three to six month project without having to fire the developer when the project is finished.

Outsourced Developers

We screen candidates though an extensive interview process that culminates in a hands-on development test where candidates build an actual working prototype. In addition to world class developers, SourceBOLD offers best-in-class project management. We find that many companies either don't have the expereince or the time to effectively manage a developer or team of developers. For this reason, SourceBOLD includes free U.S. project management time with every contract it sells. This allows you to contact a native English speaker during your hours of operation.
